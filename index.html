Respiratory system


Introduction

On this page we will talk about one of the most important systems of the human body, we talk about the "respiratory system".

To stay alive and function properly, the human body requires a sufficient amount of energy, which is provided thanks to the oxygen that enters our body, but as they say, everything that goes in must come out, well, our body is expelled as carbon dioxide and this whole procedure is thanks to the respiratory system.

if this system began to fail we would be in serious problems, since it would be at stake our lives in some cases, so we designed and explained the design of a robot capable of repairing one of the main organs of this system: "the lung", which is responsible for "storing" the oxygen entered into our body to subsequently releasing carbon dioxide.

The respiratory system, as we apply it on the next page, not only facilitates the exchange of gases between air and blood and between blood and the cells of the body, which, although it is the most important, is not its only function, It also helps us smell and create sound thanks to the air passing through our vocal cords.
